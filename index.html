Why Agri-Safe is Different

Most apps just tell you what's wrong. We tell you when and how to fix it.

Spray Smart Technology

Prevents pesticide washout. If our AI detects disease but the weather forecast predicts rain, we advise you to HOLD spraying, saving you money and protecting the soil.

  • Real-time OpenWeatherMap Integration
  • Cost-saving advisory logic

Vernacular RAG Chatbot

Powered by Google Gemini. Unlike standard chatbots, ours is grounded in official government agriculture manuals to prevent hallucinations.

  • Supports local Indian languages
  • Zero fake advice; 100% verified sources

How It Works

1. Snap a Photo

Use your phone camera to scan the affected crop leaf.

2. AI + Weather Check

MobileNetV2 identifies the disease while we fetch local weather data.

3. Get Safe Advice

Receive a "Safe to Spray" or "Wait" recommendation instantly.
